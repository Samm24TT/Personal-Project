# 🎵 BeatStrike

**Upload any MP3. Play it as a rhythm game.**

![Start Screen](screenshots/start-screen.png)

---

## What is BeatStrike?

A **4-lane browser rhythm game** that turns any MP3 into a playable level.

- 🎧 Upload any song from your library
- ⚡ Beats are detected automatically
- 🎮 Play instantly — no setup, no accounts, no downloads

---

## How to Play

**Controls:** `D` `F` `J` `K`

| Lane | Key |
|------|-----|
| Left-1 | **D** |
| Left-2 | **F** |
| Right-1 | **J** |
| Right-2 | **K** |

Hit notes as they reach the line. Time it right for **Perfect** or **Good**.

![Gameplay](screenshots/gameplay.png)

---

## Scoring System

- **Perfect** — precise timing, max points
- **Good** — close timing, partial points
- **Miss** — you let it slip, combo breaks

**Combo multipliers** increase your score with consecutive hits.

🏆 **Leaderboard** tracks your best runs per song.

![Leaderboard](screenshots/leaderboard.png)

---

## Features

- ✅ **Any MP3 works** — no custom beatmaps needed
- ✅ **100% browser-based** — no server, no uploads, no signup
- ✅ **Real-time beat detection** — powered by Web Audio API
- ✅ **Visual feedback** — lane flashes, hit effects, combo counter
- ✅ **Glassmorphism UI** — modern, clean design
- ✅ **Local leaderboard** — scores saved per song
